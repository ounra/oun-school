Oun'Schools provides a unique schooling environment for high functioning students whose needs are not being met by mainstream public schools. AEF has two distinct educational tracks (Preparatory Program: Grades K-12 for college bound students | Academy Program: Grades 5th to 12th for students who are not ready for a college prep curriculum

AEF Schools provide that alternative—a safe, supportive, and individualized learning environment where children can thrive. At the Lower Campus, we offer three distinctive programs tailored to meet the unique needs of students at various stages of their development.
